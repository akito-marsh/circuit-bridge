About FTC

What is FIRST Tech Challenge?

FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is an international robotics competition for students in grades 7–12. Teams of up to 15 members design, build, and program robots to compete in an annual game challenge — developing real skills in engineering, programming, and teamwork along the way.

What We Offer

How We Support Your Team

Circuit Bridge provides hands-on, bilingual support to help Japanese high schools get their FTC teams off the ground — from registration to their first competition season.

  • 🗣️

    Real English Exposure

    Direct communication with our active FTC team in Victoria, BC — a unique opportunity to practice English in a real technical environment.

  • 🤝

    Mentorship

    Experienced team members guide your students through robot design, programming, and competition strategy step by step.

  • 💻

    Online Guidance (Zoom & Discord)

    Regular online sessions to answer questions, review progress, and keep your team motivated throughout the season.

  • 📋

    Team Registration Support

    Bilingual step-by-step guidance through the entire FIRST registration process, so no paperwork gets in the way.
